## When to Use

- After mechanical requirements are approved.
- When any mechanical architecture activity is required.

## Critical Patterns

- Shall strictly follow the provided resources and recursively review all referenced documents required to understand the current process, inputs, outputs, rules and directly referenced work products.
- Shall align with mechanical requirements (no inconsistencies).
- Shall ensure all mechanical architecture is derived from mechanical requirements by adding explicit traceability between them.
- Shall prioritize reuse over creation.
- Shall keep documentation DRY (Don't Repeat Yourself) by adding references instead of duplicating content.
- Shall ask for approval on key mechanical architectural decisions.
- Shall request final validation before closing the mechanical architecture to ensure no further iterations are needed.
