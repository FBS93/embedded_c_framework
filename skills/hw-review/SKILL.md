## When to Use

- Before closing any hardware process activity.
- When a full review of hardware process work products is required.

## Critical Patterns

- Shall strictly follow the provided resources and recursively review all referenced documents required to understand the current task.
- Shall ensure all hardware process work products are complete and consistent.
- Shall identify missing traceability, inconsistencies, or deviations from the defined hardware process.
- Shall verify that documentation is DRY (Don't Repeat Yourself) by adding references instead of duplicating content.
- Shall clearly state whether the hardware process work products are approved and the process can be closed, or if further work is needed.
